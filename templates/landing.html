Managing your investment portfolio

should be a piece of cake

Investing can be quite intimidating and very complicated.

But it doesn't have to be.

There is no need to give up your control and be at the mercy of expensive advisors.

Get started for free

  • Understand your portfolio

    Are you as diversified as you think? Does your allocation match your long term plans? Are certain positions underperforming? See how different positions contributed to your portfolio over time. Uncorrelated investments add overall stability to your portfolio.

  • Easily compute your investment gains

    Things can get complex easily if you have sold investments you've been buying in a different currency than the one you are doing the taxes in. You also need to track how much each share cost you and deal with annoying things like stock splits. It's easy to mess this stuff up without software to help you.

  • Track your dividends, contributions and growth

    How much dividends did you get this year? How much of your portfolio growth was due to your contributions vs dividends vs investment growth? Track this easily even if you use multiple brokers.

  • Plan and execute

    Ever planned to sell a stock when it reached certain value or age? Set yourself alerts when your portfolio matches certain criteria. This can be helpful for rebalancing your portfolio, selling a value investment that now appears overpriced or optimizing your taxes.

  • Financial modeling for retirement

    When will your portfolio be enough to retire on? How much could you withdraw without risking running out of money? Sadly, Invertimo won't predict the future for you, but can help you model various scenarios and track your withdrawals.

  • Track your investing decisions

    Have you ever wondered what have you been thinking making certain trades a couple of years earlier? Save yourself future trouble and record the thoughts behind your investment moves, your ideas, strategies and speculations, so you can be more disciplined and learn from your mistakes.
