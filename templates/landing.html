Tracking your investments

should be a piece of cake

Even if you have assets in different currencies than your local one and own stocks, bonds, investment trusts or cryptos.

Invertimo will help you correctly track you gains, dividends, interests and overall performance of your investments across multiple different brokerages and exchanges in one place.

Get started for free

  • Easily compute your investment gains

    Calculating gains seems simple at first, unless you have assets that trade in different currencies than your local one, stocks split or you use more than one brokerage to trade a given asset.

    You need to track the exchange rates at the times of the transactions, stock units and the order of acquiring assets across investment accounts. This gets complicated real fast.

    On top of that in some countries such as Ireland, funds are taxed differently than stocks and you have to deal with deemed disposal and pay taxes from unrealized gains.

    Invertimo will make it all very easy for you.

    It's perfect for an European investor who has assets in Euro, USD, GBP and crypto.

    Learn more

  • Track your dividends, contributions and growth

    How much dividends did you get this year? How much of your portfolio growth was due to your contributions vs dividends vs investment growth? Track this easily even if you use multiple brokers.

  • Understand your portfolio

    Are you as diversified as you think? Does your allocation match your long term plans? Are certain positions underperforming? See how different positions contributed to your portfolio over time.
